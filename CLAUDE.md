# MusicEducation

## Purpose
A structured music education project for Michael, taught by Claude. Covers music theory fundamentals, ear training, and practical composition skills — building toward confident use of the tools in `/workspace/MusicTest/`.

## Structure
- `lessons/` — Written lesson content, organized by topic
- `exercises/` — Interactive Python scripts for ear training and practice
- `notes/` — Michael's personal notes, observations, questions
- `curriculum.md` — Master curriculum with progress tracking

## Approach
- Start from absolute zero. Assume no prior music theory knowledge.
- Every concept gets a practical audio example where possible (using MusicTest tools).
- Ear training runs in parallel with theory — hearing is as important as knowing.
- Lessons are conversational, not textbook-dry. Michael learns best through dialogue.
- Tie concepts back to the music we're creating in MusicTest whenever relevant.

## Dependencies
- Uses `/workspace/MusicTest/.venv/` and `/workspace/MusicTest/src/` for audio generation/analysis
- No separate venv needed — this is primarily a content + lightweight scripts project
